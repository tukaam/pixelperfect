# PixelPerfect Usage Guide

Welcome to **PixelPerfect**, your professional toolkit for image transformation. This guide will walk you through the core features and how to get the most out of our tools.

## Core Features

### 1. Resize Image
Easily change the dimensions of your images.
- **Upload**: Drag and drop your image or click to upload.
- **Settings**: Choose your preferred unit (Pixels, Inches, CM, MM).
- **Dimensions**: Enter the desired width and height.
- **Processing**: Click "Apply Resize" and then download your transformed image.

### 2. Compress Image
Reduce the file size of your images while maintaining quality.
- **Optimization**: Use the built-in presets for specific file size targets (e.g., 20KB, 50KB, 100KB).
- **Format**: Convert between JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats for better compression.

### 3. Social Media Templates
Save time by using pre-configured sizes for popular platforms:
- **Instagram**: Posts, Stories, Profiles.
- **Facebook**: Cover Photos, Profile Pictures, Posts.
- **YouTube**: Thumbnails, Channel Art.
- **LinkedIn**: Backgrounds, Profiles.
- **Twitter (X)**: Headers, Posts.

---

## Tips for Best Results

### Maintaining Quality
- **Aspect Ratio**: By default, the tool maintains the aspect ratio to prevent stretching.
- **High Quality**: When compressing, start with a higher quality setting and gradually decrease if needed.

### Batch Processing
- You can upload and process up to **12 images** simultaneously.
- Use the **"Download All"** button to save all processed images in one go.

### Privacy
- **100% Client-Side**: All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring total privacy.
